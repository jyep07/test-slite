# Mars

#4 planet from the Sun, known as the "Red Planet" for the iron
oxide (rust) that covers its surface.

## Overview

Mars is a cold, desert world with a thin carbon-dioxide atmosphere. Evidence of
ancient river valleys, lake beds, and minerals that form in water suggests Mars
once had liquid water and a thicker atmosphere, making it a prime target in the
search for past life.

## Notable Features

- **Olympus Mons** — the largest volcano in the Solar System, standing about
  22 km tall (nearly three times the height of Mount Everest).
- **Valles Marineris** — a canyon system over 4,000 km long.
- **Polar ice caps** made of water and frozen carbon dioxide.

## Exploration

Mars is the most explored planet beyond Earth, with active rovers including
**Curiosity** and **Perseverance**, and the **Ingenuity** helicopter — the
first powered flight on another planet.
